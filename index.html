FAQ

What is your turnaround time?

Typical projects are completed within 5–10 business days, depending on complexity and scope. Rush delivery is available upon request.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! All packages include unlimited revisions. I work closely with you until you're 100% satisfied with the final cut.

What software do you use?

I primarily work with Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve for color grading. Audio is handled in Logic Pro.

Can you work with raw footage?

Absolutely. I accept all major file formats including RAW, ProRes, H.264, and more. Just send your files securely and I'll handle the rest.
